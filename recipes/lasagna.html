Lasagna

Description

This easy lasagna recipe will save you alot of time in the kitchen. It will save you so much time and stress. Your family will love it!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb lean ground beef
  • 1 jar of spaghetti sauce
  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 1/2 cup water

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
  2. Cook beef and spaghetti sauce.
  3. Spread sauce and layer with lasagna noodles. Repeat layering.
  4. Bake for 45 minutes and let stand for 10 minutes before serving.
